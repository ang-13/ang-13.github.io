Pumpkin Crumb Cake Muffins

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: 12-14 muffins

Crumn Toppings:

Ingredients Quantity
brown sugar 1/4 cup
all-purpose flour 3/4 cup
unsalted butter 1/4 cup
pumpkin pie spice 1 tsp
unsalted melted butter 6 tsp

Pumpkin Muffins:

Ingredients Quantity
all-purpose flour 7/4 cup
ground cinnamon 2 tsps
baking soda 1 tsp
salt 1/2 tsp
pumpkin pie spice 1 tsp
canola/vegetable oil 1/2 cup
granulated sugar 1/2 cup
brown sugar 1/2 cup
canned pumpkin puree 3/2 cup
eggs(room temp) 2
milk(room temp.) 1/4 cup

Before baking:

Preheat oven to 425°F (218°C). Spray a muffin pan(recommended 12-count) with nonstick spray or line with cupcake liners.

Pumpkin muffins

Step 1: Grab 2 bowls, one large, one medium sized.

Step 2: In the large bowl, whisk the flour, baking soda, cinnamon, pumpkin pie spice, and salt together until combined. Set aside.

Step 3: In the medium bowl, whisk the oil, granulated sugar, brown sugar, pumpkin puree, eggs and milk together until combined.

Step 4: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, then fold everything together gently just until combined and no flour pockets remain.

Step 5: Spoon the batter into liners, filling them almost full.

Crump Toppings

Step 1: Whisk the flour, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and pumpkin pie spice together until combined.

Step 2: Using a fork, lightly mix in the melted butter until crumbs form. Don’t over-mix.

Step 3: Spoon crumbs evenly on top of the batter and gently press them down into the batter so they’re snug.

Step 4: Bake for 5 minutes at 425°F (218°C) then, keeping the muffins in the oven, reduce the oven temperature to 350°F (177°C). Bake for an additional 16-17 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

Side Note: You may want to allow the muffins to cool for 10 minutes in the muffin pan to not burn yourself.

Enjoy!!!

Chocolate Strawberries

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: 30 strawberries
Ingridents Quantity
Chocolate Chips 10 ounces
Fresh Strawberries w/stem 2 pounds

Equipment:

  • Parchment paper
  • Lined pan

Step 1: Wash your strawberries and dry them until no water is left on them.

Step 2: Place parchment paper on lined pan.

Step 3: Microwave the chocolate, for every 30 seconds take it out and stir it. This is to make sure the chocolate isn't burnt.

Step 4: Dip the strawberries in the chocolate holding it by the stem. When finished place on parchment paper.

Step 5: Chill the strawberries for about 15 minutes.

Enjoy!!!

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingridents Quantity
salted butter softened 1 cup
granulated sugar 1 cup
light brown sugar packed 1 cup
pure vanilla extract 2 teaspoons
large eggs 2
all-purpose flour 3 cups
baking soda 1 teaspoon
baking powder 1/2 teaspoon
sea salt 1 teaspoon
chocolate chips 2 cups

Equipment:

  • Measuring Spoons
  • Measuring Cups
  • Mixer
  • Spatula
  • 3 baking sheets
  • Parchment Paper

Before Baking:

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Line three baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.

Baking:

Step 1: In a medium bowl mix flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Set aside.

Step 2: Cream together butter and sugars until combined.

Step 3: Beat in eggs and vanilla until light (about 1 minute).

Step 4: Mix in the dry ingredients until combined.

Step 5: Add chocolate chips and mix well.

Step 6: Roll however large you like your cookies of dough at a time into balls and place them evenly spaced on your prepared cookie sheets.

Step 7: Bake in preheated oven for approximately 8-10 minutes then take them out when they are just barely starting to turn brown.

Step 8: Let them sit on the baking pan for 5 minutes before removing to cooling rack.

Enjoy!!!
